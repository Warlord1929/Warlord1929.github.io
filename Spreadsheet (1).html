The Spreadsheet/Excel assignment was from April to May 2021 and was our final assignment of the course. This assignment involved creating a spreadsheet from scratch with only certain points of the data given which we then had to interprate and put on the spreadsheet in any format we also had to meet a special criteria which involved using different operations such as + - * /on the spreadsheet we also had to use sums such as =SUM +AVERAGE and =MAX to add all of the totals together and create and average net spend among others.

In this course we learnt how to create a spreadsheet and use numerous excel operations and sums to make it easier to create the spreadsheets. We also learnt how to properly format the tables to make them look aesthetically appealing.

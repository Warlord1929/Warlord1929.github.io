This photoshop/digital imaging unit was from April to May 2021 and was our 11th overall assignment for the Level 1 Unit Course.

In this unit we learned how to edit various photos and use various tools in photoshop, the tools used include crop,lasso,object selection,eyedropper,brush,clone,eraser,text,zoom,hand,rectangle,history brush,blend,frame,spot healing brush,rectangle marquee and marquee.

For our assignment we had to create a photoshopped image that promoted fair trade using the following criteria: It had to have young people incorporated into the image in someway, The assignment brief also required us to include chocolate and the fair trade logo in the image.

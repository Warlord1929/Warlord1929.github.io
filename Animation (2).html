In this unit I learned how to motion tween,create symbols, animate images,add in audio clips and use various tools in animate such as layers and zoom.

The animation assignment for Level 1 IT was from November to December 2020 and required us to make a 20 second long animation with audio clips and motion tweens. This assignment was our 4th of the course.

For this Assignment I created an animation based on the game of Minecraft which was 20 seconds long with audio clips from star wars to further improve the quality of the animation. This assignment was quite fun to practise for and to practise for it i created two animations one a mario kart one where I screwed up one of the tweens and the other one being a Mario 64 inspired animation with no tween errors showing improvement in the field.
